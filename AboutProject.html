    About Project

    Abstract:

    The project aims to predict Emotional Intelligence (EI) in individuals based on their fluid intelligence. Emotional Intelligence plays a crucial role in personal and professional success, and understanding its relationship with fluid intelligence can provide valuable insights. The project utilizes Bloom's Taxonomy for cognitive assessment to establish a comprehensive framework for evaluating both fluid intelligence and its impact on Emotional Intelligence.

    Project Purpose:

    The primary purpose of this project is to investigate the relationship between fluid intelligence and emotional intelligence. By leveraging machine learning techniques, we aim to create a model that can predict an individual's emotional intelligence scores based on their performance in fluid intelligence tasks.

    Problem Statement:

    In a fast-paced world marked by information overload and multitasking demands, managing multiple tasks simultaneously can strain cognitive abilities, impacting performance negatively. The central objective is to create a system proficient in analyzing users' Working Load and Fluid Intelligence, correlating these with their Emotional Intelligence (EI), and providing accurate suggestions for improvement.

    Solution and Expected Result:

    The proposed solution involves the development of a machine learning model that analyzes data from fluid intelligence assessments to predict an individual's emotional intelligence. We expect the model to reveal patterns and relationships that contribute to a deeper understanding of the cognitive basis of emotional intelligence. The anticipated result is a reliable predictive tool that can estimate an individual's emotional intelligence with a high degree of accuracy.

    Project Benefits:

    Enhanced Understanding: The project aims to provide insights into the cognitive mechanisms underlying emotional intelligence, contributing to a better understanding of the relationship between fluid intelligence and emotional intelligence.

    Problem Domain:

    The business domain for this project lies at the intersection of cognitive assessment, human resources, and personal development. Organizations across various sectors, including education, recruitment, and professional training, can benefit from a reliable tool that assesses emotional intelligence based on fluid intelligence. Individuals seeking to enhance their emotional intelligence for personal and professional growth are also part of the target audience.

    The high-level technical domain encompasses the fields of machine learning, data science, and cognitive psychology. Leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, the project involves the analysis of data gathered from fluid intelligence assessments. Feature engineering, model training, and validation are critical components within the technical domain. Additionally, the project may involve the integration of the developed model into user-friendly interfaces or platforms for practical applications, such as online assessments or training programs.
